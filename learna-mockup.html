Welcome to Learna Flexible online education. Delivered by experts, designed for professionals.

Why choose Learna? We have over 10 years’ experience delivering online courses, giving you access to flexible, affordable distance learning in partnership with world-leading educators.

Affordable Education

Our courses are some of the most affordable of their kind, providing value for money without compromising on quality. We also don't apply an international surcharge.

Study Flexibly

Self-directed online learning puts you in control. No fixed timetable means you can log in & learn on the go, at your convenience.

Learn with Experts

Being totally online means we can select the very best tutors and resource editors, no matter where they’re located.

Grow Your Network

Connect with multidisciplinary people around the world to expand your insights & broaden your career prospects.

Boost Your Prospects

Fast-track your career with intensive courses that let you graduate quicker. Access CPD & exam resources anytime, anywhere.

Environmentally Conscious

Our programmes produce 90% fewer emissions than campus-based learning.
